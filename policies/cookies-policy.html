Cookies Policy

This cookies policy outlines how we use cookies on our website.

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and to provide you with personalized content and recommendations.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device by a website. They are used to store information about your interactions with the website, such as the pages you visit and the links you click on.

What Cookies Do We Use?

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Session cookies: These cookies are used to track your interactions with our website during a single session. They are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies are used to track your interactions with our website over multiple sessions. They are stored on your device for a longer period of time.

How Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies to:

  • Improve your browsing experience: We use cookies to track your interactions with our website and to provide you with personalized content and recommendations.
  • Collect analytics data: We use cookies to collect analytics data about your interactions with our website. This data is used to improve our website and to provide you with a better browsing experience.

How Do You Control Cookies?

You can control cookies by adjusting your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to delete or block cookies. You can also set your browser to notify you when a website is trying to place a cookie on your device.
